# Timed Coding Quiz

## This is a 5 question timed quiz about coding.

#  Description

## ![start screen](./readme%20imgs/start%20screen.png)

## The first page will have a short explanation for the quiz and will have the timer located at the top-right of the page. Also, you will have the option to view previous scores when you click on "View high scores" located top-left of the page.

## ![question screen](./readme%20imgs/question-screen.png)

## ![question clicked](./readme%20imgs/question-clicked.png)

## After you click the start quiz button, you will be taken to the questions. When you click your answer it will automatically go to the next question. It will show whether the answer choice was correct or wrong. And if the answer was wrong, 10 seconds will be removed from the timer.

## ![end screen](./readme%20imgs/end%20screen.png)

## When you finish the questions or the timer runs out, you will be taken to the end screen where you can view your final score and submit your initials.

## ![high scores](./readme%20imgs/high-scores.png)

## When you submit your initials, you can see your past scores.

## ![clear scores](./readme%20imgs/high-scores.png)
## ![after clear scores](./readme%20imgs/after-clear-scores.png)

## At the bottom of the page, you can clear your previous scores.

## ![go back](./readme%20imgs/go%20back.png)
## ![after go back](./readme%20imgs/start%20screen.png)

## You also have the option to go back to the start screen.
